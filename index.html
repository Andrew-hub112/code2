$$ \frac{d}{dt} t^N = N t^{N-1} $$ $$ v(t)= \frac{dx(t)}{dt} [m]/[s] $$ $$ a(t)= \frac{dv(t)}{dt} [m]/[s]^2 $$

$$ \vec{r}(t) = x(t)\hat{i} + y(t)\hat{j} [m] $$ $$ \vec{v}(t) = \frac{d\vec{r}}{dt} [m]/[s] $$ $$ \vec{a}(t) = \frac{d\vec{v}}{dt} [m]/[s]^2 $$

$$ x(t) = x_0 + v_0 \cos\theta \, t [m] $$ $$ y(t) = y_0 + v_0 \sin\theta \, t - \frac{1}{2} g t^2 [m] $$ $$ R = \frac{v_0^2 \sin(2\theta)}{g} [m] $$ $$ H = y_0 + \frac{v_0^2 \sin^2\theta}{2g} [m] $$ $$t_ymax= \frac{v0 \sin(\theta)}{g} [s]$$ $$t_{xmax}= 2\frac{v0 \sin(\theta)}{g} [s]$$
